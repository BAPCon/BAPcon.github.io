PyroResearch


GitHub Repository


Purpose

ChatGPT has been controversial lately due to its likely use for academic dishonesty in the form of AI generated essays. This project seeks a middleground approach by generating research "packages" for users.

After entering a topic, like World War 2, a "package" containing the key points, important followup questions, and possible theses for an essay is generated.

Approach

A number of queries for ChatGPT were identified like "What are the 5 most important aspects of [topic]". By adding "as a list" to the query, it is easy to parse the response into an array/list.




Outside of the essay topics, each query is sent individually, without the prior conversation included to avoid reinforcing any tainted responses.

The prior conversations are included when querying essay topics, to add better context.




Media

Non-copyrighted images are found by querying Wikimedia Commons backend, and license unspecified images are found with ScaleSerp.com's API.


Youtube videos are found using the 'youtubesearchpython' package, which works shockingly well without getting Google Cloud involved.
